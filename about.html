Meet Hope.

A techie + creative obsessed with
aesthetics & seamless experiences.

I was raised in Atlanta, Georgia but my roots are deeply ingrained within my West African culture. I graduated from the University of Georgia in May 2015, where I studied Computer Science. At a young age, I discovered my interest in art, photography, design, and coding. Since then, I have continued to water these passions. I am currently a software developer and consultant at Capgemini, where I work on cross-functional teams in a fast-paced environment.

I am interested in exploring the intersections between technology, design thinking and human computer interactions. Specifically, my goal is to explore ways to create technologies while taking into account distinct intercultural mores and behaviors.
